Recipe

Ingridients Amount
Chocolate 200g
Butter 200g
Sugar 200g
Egg 4pcs
Flour 1sp
  1. Step: Mix the chocolate pieces and butter in a bowl.
  2. Step: Stir in the sugar and allow to cool slightly.
  3. Step: Add eggs and wheat flour. Stir until smooth.
  4. Step: Pour the batter into a beaten cake tin and bake for 25 minutes in a 200-degree oven.
  5. Step: Allow the cake to cool before serving.
